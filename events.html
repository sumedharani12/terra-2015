Events

We plan, organize and set up more than 10 events that occur over the two days of terra. This year Terra aims are reinventing and refining the Events organized, by adding elements of fun, innovation and excitement. The events will allow all the participants to display their talents, skills and simultaneously test their knowledge of geology. The events organized create a healthy spirit of competitiveness among the teams.

DEBATE:

"It is better to debate a question without settling it than to settle a question without debating it" – J. Joubert.
The Debates at terra will open the minds of not only the participants but the audience as well. You may have to Debate against your opinion, but that’s what makes it challenging. Participants will have to be pertinent and brief within the limited time allotted.

TREASURE HUNT:

This event is not the simple and ordinary treasure hunt, it has a twist. Using only GPS coordinates and maps provided, participants will be expected to complete all the tasks and obstacles as they make their way to the finish point.

PHOTOGRAPHY COMPETITION:

A picture speaks a thousand words. Capture a geology related outcrop and provide it a caption. These pictures will be displayed and judged during terra.
