The first edition of the Terra magazine was released on 12th December 2012. The magazine is granted an ISSN number (ISSN 2320-298X) and the Terra is a registered trademark The previous editions of Terra brought us closer to understanding the extinction events, the volcanoes that moulded the earth to its present form as well as the evolution of life on earth. This year in its 4th edition, Terra, delves into an aspect of geosciences that is closer to the present, practical and more pragmatic-Gemmology. Being an economic and technical spinoff of Mineralogy, it forms an integral part to the study of geosciences. This is a branch that gives aspiring professionals and budding geologists an exciting prospect for bringing about socio-economic growth and innovation. This edition of Terra brings out the quirks in how we perceive this fascinating aspect of geoscience and debunks the various mysteries associated with it.
