About Us

The Department of Geology, St. Xavier’s College, is a prominent and prestigious establishment. It is among the oldest geology departments in India, founded in 1920. The main aim of our department is to secure a strong understanding and foundation in geology along with instilling curiosity and interest in the subject. It has a wide and rare collection of fossils, minerals and gemstones.
The past academic year has been rewarding as the students and staff acquired numerous laurels. The college was awarded the India Geospatial Leadership Award 2014 for capacity development on 10th February 2015 on the auspices of the India Geospatial Forum in Hyderabad, for contribution of geology to the development of geospatial science and technology in the country. The citation and trophy was accepted by Dr Hrishikesh Samant and the post graduate students of the department. On the academic front, the post graduate and undergraduate students cleared and secured ranks in various tests such as, CSIR-UGC – NET/Lecturership examination, Joint Admission Test for Masters (JAM).
